ABOUT

Clean Clode is a free, open-source text cleaning tool designed specifically for developers working with Claude Code and OpenAI Codex. Whether you're copying a prompt, terminal output, error messages, or formatted responses from VS Code/Terminal, Clean Clode intelligently removes unwanted formatting characters, box drawing elements, and excessive whitespace while preserving the meaningful content and structure. Clean Clode processes your text entirely in the browser - no uploads, no tracking, no data collection. Just clean, readable text ready for your documentation, bug reports, or personal notes.
